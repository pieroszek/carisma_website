About us

Our Mission

We aim to contribute to the social welfare of Andorra by using the profits from our stores to offer support to non-profit entities with focus especially on training and employment for people in a situation of social need and on environmental issues. In addition, we aim to promote environmental welfare by reducing waste and saving resources through recyling, reusing and upcycling.

History

Our Story

In January 2011, CARISMA started as the first charitable store with support from Comú de la Massana, providing two spaces for free in the parish of la Massana. The store became the hub of association activities, involving volunteers from various nationalities. It gained quick success, receiving recognition from public administrations. In 2012, CARISMA won the first prize in the 7th edition of the Environmental Initiatives Contest. In 2014, it was honored as Citizen of the Year by Comú de la Massana.

Over the years, CARISMA faced challenges hindering its growth, such as seeking new spaces, transportation, and repair of donated items, along with volunteer availability.

In 2014, CARISMA collaborated with the AGENTAS program of the Escola Especialitzada Nostra Senyora de Meritxell (EENSM). AGENTAS managed stores in Escales – Engordany and Sant Julià de Lòria, employing individuals with disabilities. CARISMA volunteers continued managing the Massana and l'Aldosa stores.

The "CARISMA amb AGENTAS" store in Escaldes – Engordany, and another in Sant Julià de Lòria opened in 2015 through collaborations with Comú d’Escaldes – Engordany, Andbank, Comú de Sant Julià de Lòria, and the Department of Environment of the Govern d Andorra.

In January 2018, a direct agreement was made with EENSM, leading to the opening of a new store in Andorra la Vella, "EENSM amb CARISMA," thanks to collaboration with Morabanc. How's the company doing now? Any recent developments?
